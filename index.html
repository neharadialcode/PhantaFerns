About Us

Welcome to PhantaFrens! We are glad you're here. PhantaFrens are alien-styled NFTs with a unique creation, inspired by, but not affiliated with PhantaBears and Alien Frens. By putting our own twist on these collectibles, we were able to create thousands of vibrant and colorful one-of-a-kind images for all to enjoy and collect.
